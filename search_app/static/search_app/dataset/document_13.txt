Document 13: AI in Transportation
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is significantly transforming the transportation sector, enhancing safety, efficiency, and logistics. 
AI technologies are utilized in various applications, including traffic management systems, autonomous vehicles, and route optimization. 
Smart traffic signals powered by AI analyze real-time traffic data to adjust signal timings, reducing congestion and improving travel times for commuters. 
In logistics, AI algorithms optimize delivery routes, considering factors like traffic, weather, and vehicle capacity, leading to cost savings and faster service. 
Moreover, autonomous vehicles leverage AI for navigation, object detection, and decision-making, paving the way for safer roads. 
The future of transportation is set to be shaped by AI, promising innovative solutions that enhance mobility and reduce environmental impact. 
As the technology evolves, it will continue to create new opportunities for improved transportation systems globally.
