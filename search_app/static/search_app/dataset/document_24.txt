Document 24: AI in Supply Chain Management
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing supply chain management by providing organizations with enhanced visibility and efficiency. 
AI algorithms analyze data across the supply chain, identifying patterns and trends that inform decision-making. 
This enables companies to optimize inventory levels, predict demand fluctuations, and enhance logistics operations. 
AI-powered predictive analytics help businesses anticipate disruptions, allowing them to implement proactive measures to mitigate risks. 
Moreover, AI technologies improve supplier selection processes, assessing supplier performance based on historical data. 
As supply chains become increasingly complex, AI will play a crucial role in enhancing agility and resilience, ultimately driving business success. 
The future of supply chain management is set to be characterized by data-driven decisions and AI innovations that foster efficiency and responsiveness.
